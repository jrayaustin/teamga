About

We are a community! We employ members of the communities we operate in and engage the employees of the YMCAs, Boys & Girls Clubs, and churches we drop off at.

What we do
  1. We bring fresh, locally-grown produce to families in food deserts
  2. Customers can purchase produce online or in-person and receive a walk through of our site from a staff member
  3. We accept a variety of payment options (EBT, debit, credit, cash)
  4. We provide recipes to educate users on how to use produce they may be unfamiliar with
  5. We partner with YMCAs, Boys & Girls Clubs, and churches to bring fun activities to community members
Why we do it
  1. We believe families should be informed, engaged, and encouraged on their journeys’ to healthy living
  2. We believe that no matter where you live or how you’re paying, you should have access to fresh food, it’s the real deal!
